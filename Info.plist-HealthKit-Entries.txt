========================================
COPY THESE ENTRIES TO YOUR Info.plist
========================================

Add these two entries to your Info.plist file:

Method 1: Raw XML (if editing Info.plist as source code)
---------------------------------------------------------
<key>NSHealthShareUsageDescription</key>
<string>Kura needs access to your workout and activity data to calculate calories burned and show your net calorie balance.</string>

<key>NSHealthUpdateUsageDescription</key>
<string>Kura may write nutrition data to Apple Health in the future.</string>


Method 2: Property List Editor (if using Xcode's GUI)
------------------------------------------------------
Key: NSHealthShareUsageDescription
Type: String
Value: Kura needs access to your workout and activity data to calculate calories burned and show your net calorie balance.

Key: NSHealthUpdateUsageDescription  
Type: String
Value: Kura may write nutrition data to Apple Health in the future.


QUICK SETUP CHECKLIST:
----------------------
☐ 1. In Xcode, select your app target
☐ 2. Go to "Signing & Capabilities" tab
☐ 3. Click "+ Capability" and add "HealthKit"
☐ 4. Add the two entries above to Info.plist
☐ 5. Build and run on a REAL device (not simulator)
☐ 6. Go to Profile → Apple Health in the app
☐ 7. Tap "Connect Apple Health"
☐ 8. Grant permissions in the iOS dialog

REMEMBER: HealthKit only works on real devices, NOT in the simulator!
