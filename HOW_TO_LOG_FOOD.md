## ‚ùì **Common Questions**

### **Q: How accurate is the camera scan?**
A: Very accurate! The AI estimates actual portion sizes (not just 100g defaults like other apps). For best results, use good lighting and clear photos.

### **Q: Can I edit after logging?**
A: Yes! Go to your food log, tap any item, and edit calories, portions, or delete it.

A: No, both Camera Scan and Quick Log need internet to access the AI. Manual Entry works offline.

A: You can edit any field after logging. The AI is highly accurate but you always have final control.

### **Q: Can I log past meals?**
A: Yes! When manually entering, you can change the timestamp to any time.
